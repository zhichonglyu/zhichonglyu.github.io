摘要：ChatGPT的引入改变了人们处理文本数据的方式。本文为ChatGPT设计了一种提示策略（prompting strategy），用于识别和分析财务沟通中的异常之处，重点关注S&P 500公司的电话财报会议（earnings call）。利用最新的GPT-4-Turbo模型，从25个维度对异常财务沟通进行识别和分类，分为四类：高管的异常沟通、财务分析师的异常沟通、异常内容和技术问题。大部分电话财报会议存在异常财务沟通，这与公司的某些特征相关，并随商业周期波动。股市对异常信息反应消极，交易频繁。ChatGPT等大型语言模型具有金融分析潜力，可为解读复杂文本数据及其对市场影响的经济后果提供新的见解。
Abstract: The introduction of ChatGPT has transformed the way people process textual data. This paper designs a prompting strategy for ChatGPT to identify and analyze anomalies in financial communications, focusing on earnings calls of S&P 500 companies. Using the latest GPT-4-Turbo model, unusual financial communications are identified and classified across 25 dimensions into four categories: unusual communication by executives, by financial analysts, unusual contents, and technical issues. Most earnings calls contain unusual financial communications, which correlate with certain firm characteristics and fluctuate with the business cycle. The stock market reacts negatively to unusual information, with elevated trading activity. LLMs such as ChatGPT hold potential for financial analysis, offering new insights into interpreting complex textual data and its economic consequences on the market.

数据
Data
  • S&P 500中的公司通常拥有最多的金融分析师关注，且比小公司更快地反映新信息 ($\color{blue}{Zhang,\ 2006}$)。
  • 对于小公司，数据数量和质量大幅降低，价格延迟可能过大，从而模糊了相关的定价渠道 ($\color{blue}{Hou\ and\ Moskowitz,\ 2005}$)。
  • Firms in the S&P 500 typically enjoy the largest following by financial analysts and reflect novel information sooner than smaller firms ($\color{blue}{Zhang,\ 2006}$).
  • For smaller firms the data quantity and quality are much reduced and the price delay may be too large, obscuring the pricing channel in question ($\color{blue}{Hou\ and\ Moskowitz,\ 2005}$).

Discoveries
  • 存在异常沟通的公司在多个维度上交易活动显著增加。

  • 异常沟通对公告收益有显著的负面影响。
  • Trading activity is significantly elevated for firms with unusual communication along many of the 25 identified dimensions.

- The literature has proposed trading volume as a measure for disagreement across investors.

- $\color{blue}{Hong\ and\ Stein\ (2007)}$ argue that disagreement may arise when investors possess different information sets or if new information leads them to update their beliefs.

  • Unusual communication has a negative and highly significant announcement return impact.

提示工程

Prompt Engineering

三步提示方法（判断电话财报会议是否异常以及异常特征）：
Three-step prompting approach (determine whether an earnings call is unusual and characterize the unusualness):
  • 第一步，随机向ChatGPT提供2015年至2023年间1000份问答会话记录的样本，并要求该模型判断某个问答会是否异常，如果存在异常则提供文字说明。
  • Step 1: Randomly provide ChatGPT with a sample of 1,000 Q&A session transcripts from 2015-2023, asking the model to determine whether a given Q&A session is unusual and, if so, provide a textual justification.
  • 第二步，将异常结果系统化。收集第一步中的异常理由，并提供给新prompt，令ChatGPT从中归纳出高层次类别。
  • Step 2: Systematize the unusual results. Collect the justifications from Step 1 and provide them to a new prompt, asking ChatGPT to derive high-level categories.
  • 第三步，检查以上维度的所有异常问答。
  • Step 3: Examine all Q&A sessions across the identified dimensions.

- 以特斯拉2018年第一季度电话财报会议中的异常沟通为例。

- Example: unusual communication in Tesla's Q1 2018 earnings call.

  • 表1概括了异常沟通维度，ChatGPT提供25个high-level categories，且降维至四个维度：

- 管理层沟通异常：指的是公司管理层在回答分析师提问时表现出的异常行为，如回答过于冗长、情绪化，或者含糊其辞、逃避问题，甚至出现自相矛盾的情况，显露出管理团队可能没有充分准备；

- 分析师互动异常：包括分析师提出的问题离题或重复、问题数量异常多、反复关注特定参与者或议题、频繁询问与财务无关的问题，以及缺少对关键问题的探讨；

- 沟通内容异常：涵盖公司变革、领导层变动、战略洞察、法律与监管议题讨论、运营和管理问题、对其市场行为的分析、对特定（财务或非财务）主题的详细讨论、外部事件的影响、宏观经济因素和公告等，以及对公司产品或服务的深入讨论；

- 技术难题：识别在沟通过程中遇到的技术执行或操作问题，可能会影响到信息或数据传输的准确性。

  • Table 1 summarizes the dimensions of unusual communication. ChatGPT identifies 25 high-level categories, reduced to four broader dimensions:

- Unusual executive communication: Abnormal behavior by management when responding to analyst questions, such as overly lengthy or emotional responses, vague or evasive answers, contradictions, or signs of insufficient preparation.

- Unusual analyst interaction: Off-topic or repetitive questions, an unusual number of questions, repeated focus on specific participants or topics, frequent non-financial inquiries, and a lack of critical questioning.

- Unusual content: Corporate changes, leadership transitions, strategic insights, legal and regulatory discussions, operational and management issues, market behavior analysis, detailed discussions of specific (financial or non-financial) topics, external event impacts, macroeconomic factors and announcements, and in-depth product/service discussions.

- Technical issues: Technical execution or operational problems during the call that may affect the accuracy of information or data transmission.

- Unusual communication reduces announcement returns by 35 basis points;

- Technical difficulties have a positive impact on returns;

- Both unusual communication and unusual content are associated with substantial declines in announcement returns;

- Adding SIC industry fixed effects leaves the coefficients for all categories of unusual communication virtually unchanged.
