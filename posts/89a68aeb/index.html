摘要：本文研究了ChatGPT和其他大型语言模型在使用新闻标题预测股市收益方面的潜力。通过ChatGPT判断新闻标题对公司股价所产生的积极、中性和消极作用，产生的得分与随后的每日股票收益之间显著正相关。尽管ChatGPT优于传统的情绪分析方法，然而GPT-1、GPT-2和BERT等更基本的模型无法准确预测收益，基于ChatGPT-4的长短期策略可获得最高的夏普比率，这说明收益的可预测性是复杂语言模型的一种新兴能力。此外，小盘股和大盘股均具有可预测性，这表明市场对公司新闻的反应不足。较小的股票和有坏消息的股票的可预测性更强，这与套利限制也起着重要作用的观点一致。总之，将先进大语言模型纳入投资决策更精准预测，并提高量化交易策略的性能。
Abstract: This paper investigates the potential of ChatGPT and other large language models in predicting stock market returns using news headlines. The scores produced by ChatGPT, which assess whether headlines have positive, neutral, or negative effects on stock prices, are significantly and positively correlated with subsequent daily stock returns. While ChatGPT outperforms traditional sentiment analysis methods, more basic models such as GPT-1, GPT-2, and BERT fail to predict returns accurately. The long-short strategy based on ChatGPT-4 achieves the highest Sharpe ratio, suggesting that return predictability is an emergent capability of sophisticated language models. Moreover, predictability exists for both small-cap and large-cap stocks, indicating market underreaction to firm-specific news. Predictability is stronger for smaller stocks and those with bad news, consistent with limits-to-arbitrage playing an important role. Overall, incorporating advanced LLMs into investment decisions enables more accurate predictions and enhances quantitative trading strategy performance.

文献：Paper:

Introduction

有限套利或称套利限制是指由于风险、成本、信息不对称和制度性约束等因素的影响，使得套利者的套利行为受到限制。 现有研究发现，套利限制会影响投资者的交易活动，使更多错误定价信息融入到交易中去，进而导致资产价格偏离其基本价值，加剧市场的错误定价和价格波动（Lam和Wei，2011；Gu等，2018）。
Limits-to-arbitrage refers to constraints on arbitrageurs' activities due to risk, cost, information asymmetry, and institutional restrictions. Existing research finds that these constraints affect investors' trading activities, allowing more mispricing to persist, causing asset prices to deviate from fundamental values and exacerbating market mispricing and price volatility (Lam and Wei, 2011; Gu et al., 2018).

金融经济领域，尤其是预测股市收益能力，使用LLMs仍然是一个相对未知的领域。LLMs预测金融市场走势表现如何是一个悬而未决的问题，本研究旨在通过LLMs提取新闻标题中的上下文预测股票收益方面的潜力填补空白。

The use of LLMs in financial economics, particularly for predicting stock market returns, remains relatively unexplored. How well LLMs predict financial market movements is an open question, and this study aims to fill this gap by exploring the potential of LLMs to extract contextual information from news headlines for predicting stock returns.

如$\color{blue}{Tetlock(2007)}$、$\color{blue}{Tetlock\ et\ al.(2008)}$ 和 $\color{blue}{Tetlock(2011)}$ 使用新闻和训练的算法成功预测股票收益，可能是因为组合新信息很复杂（$\color{blue}{Fedyk\ and\ Hodson,2023}$）。

Prior studies such as $\color{blue}{Tetlock(2007)}$, $\color{blue}{Tetlock\ et\ al.(2008)}$, and $\color{blue}{Tetlock(2011)}$ successfully predicted stock returns using news and trained algorithms, possibly because combining new information is complex ($\color{blue}{Fedyk\ and\ Hodson,2023}$).

具体做法：
  • 本文通过利用大语言模型分析新闻标题情绪，比较包括ChatGPT在内的各种LLM的性能，并将其与领先数据供应商提供的现有情绪分析方法进行比较；
  • 对于每个标题，使用ChatGPT来判断其对公司股价的作用是'good'、'bad'还是'neutral'，并转换为分数预测下一个交易日的股票收益；
Approach:
  • This paper analyzes news headline sentiment using LLMs, compares the performance of various LLMs including ChatGPT, and benchmarks them against existing sentiment analysis methods from leading data vendors;
  • For each headline, ChatGPT is used to determine whether its effect on the company's stock price is 'good', 'bad', or 'neutral', and the result is converted into a score to predict the next trading day's stock return;
实证发现：
  • ChatGPT优于传统情绪分析方法；
  • GPT-1、GPT-2和BERT等更基本的模型预测股票的能力较弱，ChatGPT-4观察到最高的可预测性；
  • 样本期内ChatGPT-4获得最高的夏普比率3.8，而基于ChatGPT-3.5策略的夏普比率为3.1；
  • ChatGPT可预测性存在于小盘股和大盘股以及具有积极和消极消息的股票中，市场似乎对公司新闻反应不足，这与现有文献中的观点一致；
  • 可预测性在小盘股中更为明显；
Empirical Findings:
  • ChatGPT outperforms traditional sentiment analysis methods;
  • More basic models such as GPT-1, GPT-2, and BERT exhibit weaker predictive ability, while ChatGPT-4 demonstrates the highest predictability;
  • ChatGPT-4 achieves the highest Sharpe ratio of 3.8 during the sample period, compared to 3.1 for ChatGPT-3.5;
  • ChatGPT predictability exists for both small-cap and large-cap stocks and for stocks with positive and negative news, suggesting market underreaction to firm-specific news, consistent with existing literature;
  • Predictability is more pronounced among small-cap stocks;
进一步工作：

根据以上发现，本研究提出一种新方法估计并理解模型的推理能力。利用模型解释其推理的能力，步骤如下：

  • 首先，通过将判断结果（不包括"UNKNOWN"）与第二天的已实现收益进行比较检验判断是否正确；
  • 其次，拟合模型，预测判断正确与否。
  • 第三，利用模型的特征重要性（$\color{blue}{Binsbergen\ et\ al.,2023}$）理解更能准确预测判断的概念；

- 当判断与stock purchases by insiders，earning guidance，dividends有关时，更可能正确；

- 当判断涉及partnerships和developments时，判断准确性降低；

Further Work:

Based on these findings, this study proposes a novel method to estimate and understand the model's reasoning capabilities. Leveraging the model's ability to explain its reasoning, the steps are as follows:

  • First, verify whether the judgment is correct by comparing the result (excluding "UNKNOWN") with the next day's realized returns;
  • Second, fit a model to predict whether the judgment is correct;
  • Third, use feature importance ($\color{blue}{Binsbergen\ et\ al.,2023}$) to understand which concepts best predict judgment accuracy;

- Judgments related to stock purchases by insiders, earnings guidance, and dividends are more likely to be correct;

- Judgments involving partnerships and developments tend to be less accurate;

  • 首先，使用网络抓取为所有CRSP公司收集一个全面新闻数据集；

  • 然后，将标题与知名新闻情绪分析数据提供商（RavenPack）的标题进行匹配【其后段落重点介绍与RavenPack匹配的重要意义】：

- 但不使用RavenPack增强标题；

  • 使用数据供应商的"相关性得分"，范围从0到100，指示新闻与特定公司的相关性

- 为了避免新闻重复，要求"事件相似性天数"超过90天，这确保只捕获有关公司的新信息；

- 剔除同一家公司一天内的重复和过于相似的标题：使用最优字符串对齐度量（受限Damerau-Levenstein距离）衡量标题的相似性，删除同一公司一天内相似性大于0.6的标题；

Data Cleaning
  • First, web scraping is used to collect a comprehensive news dataset for all CRSP firms;

- All news articles containing the company name or ticker symbol are searched, yielding headlines from various sources including major news agencies, financial news websites, and social media platforms;

- All news during the sample period is collected;

  • Then, headlines are matched with those from a leading news sentiment data provider (RavenPack):

- The majority (over 70%) of matched headlines correspond to press releases;

- RavenPack-enhanced headlines are not used;

- Following the preprocessing method of $\color{blue}{Jiang\ et\ al.\ (2021)}$, the merged dataset contains 67,586 headlines for 4,138 firms;

  • The data provider's "relevance score" (0 to 100) indicating news relevance to a specific firm is used:

- Only news with a relevance score of 100 is retained;

- Restricted to full articles and press releases, excluding headlines classified as "stock rises" and "stock falls" (which only indicate the daily stock movement direction);

- To avoid news duplication, "event similarity days" exceeding 90 days is required, ensuring only new information about firms is captured;

- Duplicate and overly similar headlines for the same firm on the same day are removed using an optimal string alignment metric (restricted Damerau-Levenshtein distance), deleting headlines with similarity greater than 0.6;

Headlines are matched with the next trading session. For headlines before 6 AM on a trading day, the position is entered at the opening and exited at the close of the same day. Linear regressions of next-day stock returns on ChatGPT scores, data vendor sentiment scores, and other LLM scores are then performed. All results are therefore out-of-sample.

Results

Long-Short Strategies based on ChatGPT Scores

  • 为了评估ChatGPT预测股价走势的能力，首先考察了基于ChatGPT新闻标题得分形成的多空策略的表现。形成零成本投资组合，在新闻发布后购买得分为正的股票，并出售得分为负的股票。

- 如果消息在交易日早上6点之前发布，我们在开盘时进入头寸，在当天收盘时退出；

- 如果消息是在早上6点之后但在收盘前发布的，将以当天的收盘价进入头寸，并在下一个交易日收盘时退出；

- 如果消息是在市场收盘后宣布的，在下个工作日开盘价进入头寸，并在当日收盘时退出；

- All strategies are rebalanced daily.

  • To evaluate ChatGPT's ability to predict stock price movements, the study first examines the performance of long-short strategies formed on ChatGPT headline scores. Zero-cost portfolios are constructed by buying stocks with positive scores and selling stocks with negative scores after news release.

- If news is released before 6 AM on a trading day, the position is entered at the opening and exited at the close;

- If news is released after 6 AM but before market close, the position is entered at the close and exited at the next trading day's close;

- If news is released after market close, the position is entered at the next business day's opening and exited at that day's close;

- All strategies are rebalanced daily.

Interpretability

Evaluating ChatGPT’s Reasoning Capabilities

传统的金融机器学习模型主要关注预测，往往缺乏可解释性。
Traditional machine learning models in finance primarily focus on prediction, often lacking interpretability.
相比之下，像ChatGPT这样的大语言模型（LLMs）以自然语言提供预测和相关解释。这一独特特征为每个预测背后的原理提供了更深入的洞察，而这是传统模型基本缺乏的能力。受此独特属性的启发，本文设计了一种新的框架来利用这些定性洞察以提高预测准确性。
In contrast, large language models (LLMs) like ChatGPT offer predictions and associated explanations in natural language. This distinctive feature provides a deeper insight into the rationale behind each prediction, a capability largely absent in conventional models. Motivated by this unique attribute, we devise a novel framework to harness these qualitative insights for enhanced predictive accuracy.

相较于$\color{red}{提供有限透明度的传统定量模型}$，ChatGPT以通俗易懂的语言$\color{red}{呈现文本推理}$，增加了丰富的$\color{red}{定性信息层}$。

Unlike traditional quantitative models offering $\color{red}{limited\ transparency}$, ChatGPT presents $\color{red}{contextual\ reasoning}$ in plain language, adding a rich layer of $\color{red}{qualitative\ information}$.

假设：
Hypothesis:
  • 初始阶段集中完善ChatGPT-4的文本解释，提取得分的原始解释文本，将定性陈述与分数分离。
  • 剔除"是"、"否"和"未知"等明确的情绪指标，指标会降低情绪的定性深度；
  • 主要关注模型基本原理的本质，删除预测得分，可为彻底分析奠定基础；
  • 目的将ChatGPT的解释转化为更可量化的格式：

- 剔除中性分数，对积极或消极的原因解释进行细分；

- 分割后可以更集中分析，支持我们的假设（即如果涉及乐观或悲观的预测，解释的正确性不同）。

  • 接下来，将定性数据转换为结构化的定量数据进行回归分析：

- 采用词频-逆文档频率（TF-IDF）技术；

- $\color{red}{独特术语}$极其重要性，因为其突出了可能表达强烈市场情绪和潜在股票走势的词语。

  • The initial phase focuses on refining ChatGPT-4's textual explanations, extracting the raw explanation text, and separating qualitative statements from scores.
  • Explicit sentiment indicators such as "yes," "no," and "unknown" are removed, as they reduce the qualitative depth of sentiment;
  • The focus is on the essence of the model's rationale; removing prediction scores lays the foundation for thorough analysis;
  • The goal is to convert ChatGPT's explanations into a more quantifiable format:

- Neutral scores are excluded, and positive/negative explanations are segmented;

- This segmentation enables more focused analysis, supporting the hypothesis that the correctness of explanations differs depending on whether the prediction is optimistic or pessimistic.

  • Next, qualitative data is converted into structured quantitative data for regression analysis:

- Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) is employed;

- $\color{red}{Unique\ terms}$ are critically important, as they highlight words that may express strong market sentiment and potential stock movements.

$\color{purple}{图4}$、$\color{purple}{图5}$、$\color{purple}{图6}$和$\color{purple}{图7}$分别展示了所有、积极、消极和中性解释TF-IDF总分最高的单词。

$\color{purple}{Figures\ 4}$, $\color{purple}{5}$, $\color{purple}{6}$, and $\color{purple}{7}$ display the words with the highest aggregate TF-IDF scores for all, positive, negative, and neutral explanations, respectively.

然而，此方法无法区分解释的正确与否，因此使用有监督方法表征最能预测给定解释是否与结果正确相关的单词。
  • 将文本解释转换为结构化数据后，使用逻辑回归模型（选择逻辑回归是由于二元结果变量：股价是否朝着ChatGPT预测的方向移动），用以核实定性解释的预测能力；
  • 通过训练积极和消极新闻解释的不同模型，旨在捕捉乐观和悲观理论如何影响股价动态的微妙变化，以此提供对市场情绪驱动行为更全面的看法；
However, this method cannot distinguish between correct and incorrect explanations. Therefore, a supervised approach is used to characterize the words that best predict whether a given explanation relates correctly to the outcome.
  • After converting textual explanations into structured data, a logistic regression model is used (logistic regression is chosen due to the binary outcome variable: whether the stock price moved in the direction predicted by ChatGPT) to verify the predictive power of qualitative explanations;
  • By training separate models for positive and negative news explanations, the aim is to capture how optimistic and pessimistic rationales differently affect stock price dynamics, providing a more comprehensive view of sentiment-driven market behavior;
如何理解个体特征的意义和影响至关重要：
  • 首先提取逻辑回归模型中系数最高和最低的项（这种方法提供了正系数和负系数），以识别对解释是否正确影响最大的单词；
  • 根据每组单词对预测准确性的影响被分为"正确"和"不正确"，可揭示ChatGPT所依赖的词典，并说明某些术语如何与特定思想更加一致；
Understanding the significance and impact of individual features is crucial:
  • The terms with the highest and lowest coefficients in the logistic regression model are extracted (yielding both positive and negative coefficients) to identify words with the greatest influence on whether an explanation is correct;
  • Each set of words is categorized as "correct" or "incorrect" based on its impact on prediction accuracy, revealing the lexicon that ChatGPT relies upon and illustrating how certain terms align more closely with specific reasoning patterns;

虽然单个单词很有趣，但脱离上下文，难以理解其全部影响：

  • 为了更深入地研究有影响力单词的上下文语境，接着确定伴随目标单词的单词；
  • 通过为平均TF-IDF设置阈值百分位数过滤不太重要的单词，确保附带的单词列表既相关又有影响力；
  • 这样不仅孤立地看待单词，而且了解它们在更广泛的背景下的意义，从而揭示支撑股市预测的复杂关系网；

While individual words are interesting, their full impact is difficult to understand out of context:

  • To investigate the contextual environment of influential words, accompanying words are identified;
  • Less important words are filtered by setting a threshold percentile for average TF-IDF, ensuring that the accompanying word lists are both relevant and impactful;
  • This approach examines words not in isolation but in their broader context, uncovering the complex network of relationships underpinning stock market predictions;

Conclusion

本研究使用新闻标题的情绪分析研究ChatGPT和其他大语言模型在预测股市回报方面的潜力，新发现如下：
  • 首先，新闻标题的ChatGPT得分可以预测后续每日股票收益，可预测性优于领先数据供应商的传统情绪分析方法；
  • 其次，GPT-1、GPT-2和BERT等更基本的LLMs无法准确预测收益，基于ChatGPT-4的策略提供了最高的夏普比率，表明回报可预测性是复杂语言模型的一种新兴能力。
  • 第三，ChatGPT得分的可预测性同时出现在小盘股和大盘股中，表明市场对公司新闻的反应不足；
  • 第四，可预测性在小盘股和有坏消息的股票中更强，与套利限制一致也起着重要作用；
  • 最后，提出了一种新的方法来评估和理解模型的推理能力；
  • 通过展示LLMs在金融经济学中的价值，为越来越多的关于人工智能和自然语言处理在该领域应用的文献做出了贡献；
This study investigates the potential of ChatGPT and other LLMs in predicting stock market returns using news headline sentiment analysis. Key findings are as follows:
  • First, ChatGPT scores from news headlines can predict subsequent daily stock returns, outperforming traditional sentiment analysis methods from leading data vendors;
  • Second, more basic LLMs such as GPT-1, GPT-2, and BERT cannot accurately predict returns, while ChatGPT-4-based strategies provide the highest Sharpe ratio, suggesting that return predictability is an emergent capability of sophisticated language models;
  • Third, ChatGPT score predictability exists for both small-cap and large-cap stocks, indicating market underreaction to firm-specific news;
  • Fourth, predictability is stronger for small-cap stocks and stocks with bad news, consistent with limits-to-arbitrage playing an important role;
  • Finally, a novel method to evaluate and understand the model's reasoning capabilities is proposed;
  • By demonstrating the value of LLMs in financial economics, the study contributes to the growing literature on AI and NLP applications in this field;

本研究对未来的研究启示如下：

  • 首先，强调继续探索和开发专门为金融业量身定制的LLM的重要性，随着人工智能驱动的金融的发展，可以设计更复杂的模型来提高金融决策过程的准确性和效率；
  • 其次，未来的研究可更集中理解LLMs获得预测能力的机制上，通过确定促成ChatGPT成功预测股市收益等模型的因素，研究人员可以制定更有针对性的策略来改进这些模型，并最大限度地提高其在金融中的效用；
  • 此外，随着LLMs在金融行业越来越普遍，有必要调查其对市场动态的潜在影响，包括价格形成、信息传播和市场稳定性。未来的研究可以探索LLM在塑造市场行为中的作用及其对金融系统的潜在积极和消极影响；
  • 最后，未来的研究可以探索LLMs与其他机器学习技术和定量模型的集成，以创建结合不同方法优势的混合系统。通过利用各种方法的互补能力，研究人员可以进一步增强人工智能驱动模型在金融生态经济学中的预测能力；
Implications for future research are as follows:
  • First, the importance of continuing to explore and develop LLMs tailored specifically for the financial industry is emphasized. As AI-driven finance evolves, more sophisticated models can be designed to improve the accuracy and efficiency of financial decision-making;
  • Second, future research can focus on understanding the mechanisms through which LLMs acquire predictive capabilities. By identifying the factors behind successful models like ChatGPT, researchers can develop more targeted strategies to improve these models and maximize their utility in finance;
  • Furthermore, as LLMs become more prevalent in the financial industry, it is necessary to investigate their potential impact on market dynamics, including price formation, information dissemination, and market stability. Future research can explore the role of LLMs in shaping market behavior and their potential positive and negative effects on the financial system;
  • Finally, future research can explore the integration of LLMs with other machine learning techniques and quantitative models to create hybrid systems that combine the strengths of different approaches. By leveraging the complementary capabilities of various methods, researchers can further enhance the predictive power of AI-driven models in financial economics;

简言之，本研究证明了ChatGPT在预测股市收益方面的价值，为未来研究LLMs在金融行业的应用和影响铺平了道路。随着人工智能驱动的金融领域的不断扩展，从这项研究中收集到的见解有助于指导开发更准确、高效和可信的模型，以提高金融决策过程的效率。

In summary, this study demonstrates the value of ChatGPT in predicting stock market returns and paves the way for future research on LLM applications and implications in the financial industry. As the AI-driven financial landscape continues to expand, insights from this study can help guide the development of more accurate, efficient, and trustworthy models to enhance financial decision-making processes.
