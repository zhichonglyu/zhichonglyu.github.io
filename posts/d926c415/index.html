Software | R | Stemming and lemmatization软件 | R | 词干提取与词形还原

Created2024-02-07|Updated2026-03-06|Software
|Word Count:1.2k|Reading Time:4mins|Post Views:

内容摘自论文《词形还原方法及实现工具比较分析》

词形还原（lemmatization），是把一个任何形式的语言词汇还原为一般形式（能表达完整语义）

词干提取（stemming）是抽取词的词干或词根形式（不一定能够表达完整语义）。

词形还原和词干提取是词形规范化的两类重要方式，都能够达到有效归并词形的目的，二者既有联系也有区别

Content excerpted from the paper "Comparative Analysis of Lemmatization Methods and Tools."

Lemmatization reduces any word form to its base form (which carries complete semantics). Stemming extracts the stem or root form of a word (which may not carry complete semantics).

Lemmatization and stemming are two important approaches to word form normalization. Both can effectively consolidate word forms, but they have both similarities and differences.

Similarities

现将共同点和联系总结为以下4方面：

（1）目标一致。 词干提取和词形还原的目标均为将词的屈折形态或派生形态简化或归并为词干（stem）或原形的基础形式，都是一种对词的不同形态的统一归并的过程。

（2）结果部分交叉。 词干提取和词形还原不是互斥关系，其结果是有部分交叉的。一部分词利用这两类方法都能达到相同的词形转换效果。如"dogs"的词干为"dog"，其原形也为"dog"。

（3）主流实现方法类似。 目前实现词干提取和词形还原的主流实现方法均是利用语言中存在的规则或利用词典映射提取词干或获得词的原形。

（4）应用领域相似。 主要应用于信息检索和文本、自然语言处理等方面，二者均是这些应用的基本步骤。

The similarities can be summarized in four aspects:

    • Same objective. Both stemming and lemmatization aim to simplify or consolidate inflected or derived word forms into their stem or base form.
    • Partially overlapping results. Stemming and lemmatization are not mutually exclusive — some words yield the same result with both methods. For example, "dogs" has stem "dog" and lemma "dog."
    • Similar mainstream implementation. Both primarily rely on rule-based transformations or dictionary mappings.
    • Similar application domains. Both are foundational steps in information retrieval, text processing, and natural language processing.

Differences

二者的区别归纳为以下5方面：

（1）原理不同。 词干提取主要是采用"缩减"的方法，将词转换为词干，如将"cats"处理为"cat"，将"effective"处理为"effect"。而词形还原主要采用"转变"的方法，将词转变为其原形，如将"drove"处理为"drive"，将"driving"处理为"drive"。

（2）复杂性不同。 词干提取方法相对简单，词形还原则需要返回词的原形，需要对词形进行分析，不仅要进行词缀的转化，还要进行词性识别。词性标注的准确率也直接影响词形还原的准确率，因此，词形还原更为复杂。

（3）实现方法侧重不同。 词干提取的实现方法主要利用规则变化进行词缀的去除和缩减。词形还原则更依赖于词典，进行词形变化和原形的映射，生成词典中的有效词。

（4）结果不同。 词干提取的结果可能并不是完整的、具有意义的词，如"revival"词干提取的结果为"reviv"。而词形还原处理后获得的结果是完整的词，一般为词典中的有效词。

（5）应用领域侧重不同。 词干提取更多被应用于信息检索领域，如Solr、Lucene等，粒度较粗。词形还原更主要被应用于文本挖掘、自然语言处理，用于更细粒度、更为准确的文本分析。

The differences can be summarized in five aspects:

    • Different principles. Stemming uses a "reduction" approach (e.g., "cats" → "cat", "effective" → "effect"), while lemmatization uses a "transformation" approach (e.g., "drove" → "drive", "driving" → "drive").
    • Different complexity. Stemming is relatively simple. Lemmatization requires morphological analysis and part-of-speech identification, making it more complex.
    • Different implementation focus. Stemming relies primarily on rule-based affix removal. Lemmatization relies more on dictionary lookups and morphological mappings.
    • Different results. Stemming may produce incomplete word fragments (e.g., "revival" → "reviv"), while lemmatization always returns valid dictionary words.
    • Different application emphasis. Stemming is more commonly used in information retrieval (e.g., Solr, Lucene) at coarser granularity. Lemmatization is preferred in text mining and NLP for finer-grained, more accurate analysis.

相对而言，词干提取是简单的轻量级的词形归并方式，最后获得的结果为词干，并不一定具有实际意义。词形还原处理相对复杂，获得结果为词的原形，能够承载一定意义，与词干提取相比，更具有研究和应用价值。

In summary, stemming is a simpler, lightweight approach that may not produce meaningful words, while lemmatization is more complex but yields complete base forms with semantic meaning, making it more valuable for research and applications.
